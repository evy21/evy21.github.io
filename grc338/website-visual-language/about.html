About us

Welcome to "Sunrise Cafe," where every morning begins with a burst of flavor and warmth!

Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, Sunrise Cafe is more than just a brunch spot; it's a community hub where friends gather, families bond, and strangers become regulars.

Established in 1990, our passion for exceptional food, friendly service, and vibrant ambiance has made us a beloved fixture in the local culinary scene. From our cozy booths to our sunlit patio, every corner of Sunrise Cafe is designed to make you feel right at home.

Our culinary team, led by Chef Chris, is dedicated to crafting dishes that celebrate the freshest ingredients sourced from local farms and markets. Whether you're craving classic brunch staples like fluffy pancakes and eggs Benedict or seeking adventurous flavors in our signature dishes, our menu has something to tantalize every palate.

Thank you for choosing Sunrise Cafe as your brunch destination. We look forward to serving you and becoming part of your cherished brunch traditions for years to come.
